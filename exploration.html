Exploration

  • When a period of 112 years, four sponsors claimed land for settlers(1497-1609)
    • John Cabot - England
      • Known for exploring Canada and travelling further down South
      • Final journey believed to have been in Chesepeake Bay and further south
        • If true, NJ’s first exploration
    • Giovanni de Verrazano - France
      • Wanted to find a route to Pacific
      • Voyagees led to Americas
      • Mapped out North Carolina up to Newfoundland
      • Discovered New York Harbor along the way
    • Estevan Gomez - Spain
      • Wanted to find Spice Islands but ended up in Northeast America
      • Explored many bays and bodies of water along his routes
      • Mapped out entire Northeast America
      • Travels from Nova Scotia to New York Harbor
      • Names to now well known Hudson River first: “El Rio San Antonio”
    • Henry Hudson - Holland
      • Hired by Dutch East India Company to find route to Asia
      • Ended up in the North America region
      • First landfall Newfoundland, then Cape Cod
      • In trying to find a route to the Pacific found Delaware Bay
      • Traveled north then along Sandy Hook and Upper New York Bay
      • Sailed up the Hudson(the “passage” to the Pacific) until waters became too shallow

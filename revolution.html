Revolution Roles

  • Most skirmishes followed Battle of Monmouth in 1778
  • Pleasant Valley often horded by British for supplies
    • Because of intense attacks, the area earned a reputation as the “Hornet’s Nest
  • Beacon Hill one of three sites to warn Continental army if enemy ever came to attack
  • Fights also happened in the Montrose area as the British tried to retreat to boats in Sandy Hook
  • British also depleted livestock in the woods
    • Once, there was a great deer drive organized to eliminate deer from the northern part of the county to prevent the enemy from depleting resources

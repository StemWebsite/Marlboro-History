Extra Info

  • Cell Phone Ban
    • In 2000, Marlboro became first township to ban phone use while driving in NJ
    • Also, they were one of the leading pioneers in phone ban in the US
  • Marlboro Township Municipal Utilities Authority and 40% Green
    • MTMUA built and put into use a 900kW solar array in order to meet 40% of electricity needs by clean power
    • One of the largest in the East
    • Will reduce NJ CO2 emissions by 1,900,000 kg alongside other emissions
